Privacy Policy (English)

1. Introduction

At Hypersoft, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Hypersoft Cache Eraser extension works, which permissions it uses, and how user data is handled.

2. Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. All processes occur locally within the users browser.

3. Use of Permissions

  • browsingData: used only to clear cache and local browsing data when requested by the user.
  • storage: used only to save local extension settings (no data is shared externally).
  • activeTab: required to perform cleaning actions on the active tab when requested by the user.
  • host permissions: limited to the necessary functionality to identify and clear data from Hypersoft Medical Admin.

4. Data Sharing

The extension does not transmit information to external servers or share data with third parties.
